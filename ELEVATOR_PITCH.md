# UpQ — CEO Elevator Pitch

Most people don't choose one task manager — they're stuck with three. Your work team is on Microsoft To Do, your personal life runs through Google Tasks, and your iPhone is full of Apple Reminders. None of them talk to each other, so nothing is ever in one place, and the most important thing you need to do right now is buried somewhere across all of them.

UpQ solves that. We're a unified task intelligence layer that connects to the task apps you already use — Microsoft, Google, and Apple — and surfaces them through a single interface. But we don't just aggregate: every task you own gets automatically classified as **Now**, **Not Now**, or **Later**, server-side, so whatever client you're on — web, mobile, watch — you always know exactly what to work on next.

For developers and teams, we expose a clean REST API so any product can pull pre-classified tasks without reimplementing the logic. Credentials are encrypted, accounts are fully isolated, and the service runs on a simple $8/month subscription with a free trial.

We're not asking you to change the apps you use. We're making them all work like one. That's UpQ.
