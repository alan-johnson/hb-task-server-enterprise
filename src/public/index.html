UpQ — Unified Task Server

UpQ connects your Google Tasks and Microsoft To Do accounts through a single, secure API — letting you and your AI tools manage tasks across every provider from one place.

  • View and manage tasks from Google Tasks and Microsoft To Do in one unified interface
  • Connect AI assistants (such as Claude) and productivity tools via a standardized task API
  • Authorize once with Google or Microsoft — UpQ works on your behalf without storing task content
  • Enterprise-grade security: OAuth tokens encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM, passwords bcrypt-hashed
How we use your data: UpQ requests read/write access to Google Tasks (auth/tasks scope) and Microsoft To Do (Tasks.ReadWrite) solely to display, create, and complete tasks at your explicit request. We never store your task content, sell your data, or share it with third parties for any purpose unrelated to providing this service.
