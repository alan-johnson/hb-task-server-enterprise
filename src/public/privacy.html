This Privacy Policy describes how Handsbreadth ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Unified Task Server service (the "Service") at [YOUR DOMAIN].

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you register, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address (optional)
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never store your plain-text password)

OAuth tokens. When you connect a third-party task provider (Microsoft Tasks or Google Tasks), we store the OAuth access token and refresh token issued by that provider. These tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption and are used solely to fulfill requests you make to the Service.

Task data. When you request your tasks, lists, or other task-related data, we retrieve it from the connected provider on your behalf and return it to you. We do not persistently store your task content, list names, or any task metadata in our databases.

Usage data. We may log standard server access information (IP address, request path, timestamp) for security monitoring and troubleshooting. These logs are not shared with third parties and are retained for a maximum of [LOG RETENTION PERIOD, e.g. 30 days].

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session

  • To detect and prevent security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

Handsbreadth's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We access your Google Tasks data only to display, create, and complete tasks at your explicit request.
  • We do not use your Google data for any purpose other than providing the task management Service to you.
  • We do not transfer your Google data to third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., returning data to your authenticated session).
  • We do not use your Google data for serving advertisements or for any other commercial purpose unrelated to improving the task management functionality.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google data unless you have given us explicit permission, or it is necessary for security purposes such as investigating abuse.

4. Microsoft Graph API Data

When you connect Microsoft Tasks, we request the following Microsoft Graph API permissions:

  • Tasks.ReadWrite — to read and write your Microsoft To Do tasks
  • offline_access — to obtain a refresh token so you do not need to re-authorize repeatedly
  • User.Read — to identify your Microsoft account for API calls

We use this access solely to fulfill your task management requests. We do not share Microsoft account data with any third party.

5. Apple Reminders

Apple Reminders integration operates locally via macOS system APIs. No Apple account credentials or Reminders data are transmitted to or stored on our servers.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted at [HOSTING PROVIDER / REGION].
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM before being written to the database.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (cost factor 10) and are never stored in recoverable form.
  • All connections to the Service are encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS).
  • We restrict access to personal data to authorized personnel only.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
